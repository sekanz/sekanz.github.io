## 📊 How It Works

### Real vs Virtual Money

```
Your Checking Account: $8,000  ←─── REAL (actual money)
  ├─ Car Fund: $200           ←─── VIRTUAL (goal allocation)
  ├─ Furniture: $500          ←─── VIRTUAL (goal allocation)
  └─ Emergency: $1,500        ←─── VIRTUAL (goal allocation)

Unallocated: $5,800 (not yet assigned to goals)
```

### Automatic Paycheck Processing

```
Paycheck arrives → Deposits to real account → Allocates to goals → Updates next date
```

### Example Transaction Flow

```
You earn $3,000 paycheck
  ↓
Deposits to Checking (now $8,000)
  ↓
Allocates $200 to Car Fund
  ↓
Next paycheck scheduled (14 days later)
  ↓
You spend $150 from Car Fund
  ↓
Car Fund drops to $50, Checking drops to $7,850
  ↓
All recorded in transaction history
```

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

**Tip 1: Testing Without Waiting**
- Click "Process Now" to simulate paycheck immediately
- Useful for testing allocation rules

**Tip 2: Custom Allocations**
- Use JSON in income form for complex distributions
- Example: `[{"virtualAccountId":"id1","amount":500}]`

**Tip 3: Multiple Income Sources**
- Add both spouses' salaries
- Different frequencies? No problem (each processes independently)

**Tip 4: Regular Backups**
- Periodic screenshots of key screens
- Export data (future feature)
- Don't rely on browser storage alone

**Tip 5: Fresh Start**
- Delete accounts/goals you don't need
- Or use `localStorage.clear()` in console for complete reset
