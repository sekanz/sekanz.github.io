Loan Comparer
Mortgage Comparer lets you compare multiple mortgages. Enter loan details (Asset value, Down payment, Interest rate, Loan term, and optional extra payment), then click Add to create a mortgage column. Click the blue 'L' button to view the amortization schedule. You can modify the extra payment amount for each monthly payment in the schedule, and the loan will automatically recalculate when you click away from the extra payment field.
